### The Crisis: What Happened and Why

Silicon Valley Bank collapsed after a bank run caused by losses on long-term bonds and rapid withdrawals from tech startups.

---

### Timeline of the Crisis

* **March 8, 2023:** SVB announces losses  
* **March 10, 2023:** Bank collapses  
* **Following days:** Contagion fears spread  

---

### Winners and Losers

<WinnerBox>
- **Large Banks:** Benefited from flight to safety  
- **Treasuries:** Safe-haven demand increased  
</WinnerBox>

<LoserBox>
- **Regional Banks:** Faced heavy selling  
- **Tech Startups:** Lost key banking partner  
</LoserBox>

---

### Lessons for Investors

* 🏦 **Liquidity risk can trigger collapse quickly**  
* ⚠️ **Bank runs can spread fear rapidly**  
* 🧠 **Risk management is critical in financial systems**
