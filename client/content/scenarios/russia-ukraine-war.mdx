### The Crisis: What Happened and Why

Russia’s invasion of Ukraine disrupted global supply chains and triggered sanctions, leading to volatility in global markets and spikes in energy prices.

---

### Timeline of the Crisis

* **Feb 24, 2022:** Russia invades Ukraine  
* **March 2022:** Oil prices surge  
* **Spring 2022:** Sanctions intensify  

---

### Winners and Losers

<WinnerBox>
- **Energy Companies:** Oil and gas prices surged  
- **Defense Stocks:** Increased military spending  
</WinnerBox>

<LoserBox>
- **European Markets:** Energy dependency caused instability  
- **Global Equities:** Uncertainty led to declines  
</LoserBox>

### Lessons for Investors

* 🌍 **Geopolitical risk impacts markets instantly**  
* 🛢 **Commodities spike during conflict**  
* ⚖️ **Global diversification is essential**
